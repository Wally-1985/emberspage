Our Story

Built around one oven, one fire, one craft.

Embers and Ash started with a simple idea: real woodfired pizza shouldn't be stuck in one building. So we built a stall that travels — a hand-fired oven mounted on its own trailer, set up under our gazebo at weddings, backyards, breweries and festivals across Brisbane, bringing the smell of hardwood smoke and the sound of a hot oven straight to the party.

Every base is hand-stretched to order and slid into coals pushing 400°C, so your guests get a pizza that's blistered, smoky and ready in under two minutes — not a tray that's been sitting under a heat lamp.
